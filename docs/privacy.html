Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Lubb Writer is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we collect:

  • Text you select: When you use the extension to enhance text, the selected text is sent to our API for processing. This text is only used to provide the enhancement service you request.
  • Extension settings: Your preferences (theme, default mode, etc.) are stored locally in your browser.
  • API credentials: Your API token is stored locally in your browser and is never sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

The text you select is only used for the immediate purpose of providing the text enhancement service. We do not:

  • Store your text after enhancement is complete
  • Use your text for training machine learning models
  • Share your text with third parties
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Display ads based on your usage

3. Data Retention

Your selected text is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers after the enhancement is complete. Enhancement history stored locally in your browser can be cleared at any time through the extension settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Lubb Writer uses third-party AI services (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) to process text enhancement requests. Your text is sent to these services with your API credentials. Please review the privacy policies of your chosen AI provider.

5. Cookies

Lubb Writer does not use cookies. All data is stored locally in your browser's storage.

6. Security

Your API credentials and settings are stored locally in your browser and are protected by your browser's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong, unique API token
  • Keeping your browser and extensions up to date
  • Using browser security features and extensions

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View your settings at any time through the extension popup
  • Delete: Remove enhancement history and settings through the extension
  • Control: Disable or uninstall the extension at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Lubb Writer is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@lubbwriter.com
  • GitHub: Create an issue in our repository
